

Data Analytics for Supermarket Growth: A Sales Forecasting Project 📊✨


🚀 Project Objectives:
 The primary aim was to provide store managers and executives with a clear view of performance while utilizing time-series analysis to forecast sales for the next 15 Days.

💡 Key Features & KPIs:
Comprehensive Dashboards: Designed an intuitive and interactive interface with dynamic filtering to explore data across different regions and categories.
Revenue & Profitability: Tracked Total Revenue and Profit Margins to identify high-performing segments.
Sales Trends: Monitored Year-over-Year (YoY) and Month-over-Month (MoM) growth rates to understand seasonal fluctuations.
Customer Insights: Analyzed Average Order Value (AOV) to better understand purchasing behavior.
Advanced Forecasting: Implemented time-series models to predict sales 15 Days into the future, enabling better inventory and resource planning.

🛠️ Tools & Techniques:
Power BI: Data visualization, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), and dashboard design.
Data Transformation: Cleaning and modeling complex datasets to ensure accuracy.
Time-Series Analysis: Leveraging historical data for predictive modeling.

📈 The Impact:
This project demonstrates how data-driven forecasting can reduce uncertainty in business planning, identify underperforming categories, and highlight opportunities for revenue growth.
